The Trust Crisis in Online Interviews

As the world shifted to remote work and online assessments, a fundamental problem emerged: how do you ensure fairness and integrity when you can't physically supervise candidates?

The COVID-19 pandemic accelerated the adoption of online interviews and remote assessments across industries. However, this digital transformation came with an unexpected challenge: the complete breakdown of supervision and integrity monitoring.

Companies and educational institutions found themselves in a paradox - they needed to conduct fair assessments remotely, but had no reliable way to ensure candidates weren't cheating or being assisted by others. This created an environment of doubt and unfairness that undermined the entire assessment process.

The Core Problem

Without proper monitoring, online interviews became a "trust-but-cannot-verify" situation, putting honest candidates at a disadvantage and making results unreliable.

Technical Innovation & Smart Architecture

Strategic design decisions that solve complex problems efficiently and cost-effectively.

‚ö°

Browser-Based AI Processing

The Innovation: Instead of sending heavy video streams to powerful servers, all AI processing happens directly in the candidate's browser using TensorFlow.js and MediaPipe.

Why This Matters: Reduces server costs by 95%, eliminates bandwidth bottlenecks, ensures privacy (video never leaves the device during processing), and provides instant real-time feedback.

üéØ

Smart Event-Only Transmission

The Innovation: Only tiny event messages are sent to the server in real-time (e.g., "10:32:05 - Cell Phone Detected"), not the entire video stream.

Why This Matters: Enables real-time monitoring with minimal bandwidth usage, maintains system responsiveness even on slow connections, and creates precise audit trails.

üõ°Ô∏è

Privacy-First Architecture

The Innovation: Video analysis happens locally, full video is only uploaded at session end, and all data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Why This Matters: Addresses privacy concerns of candidates and organizations, complies with data protection regulations, and builds trust through transparent data handling.

üìä

Intelligent Integrity Scoring

The Innovation: Automated calculation of integrity scores based on the frequency, severity, and duration of flagged events during the session.

Why This Matters: Provides interviewers with actionable insights at a glance, enables objective comparison between candidates, and reduces subjective bias in evaluation.

Solution: Optimized TensorFlow.js models, efficient memory management, and WebGL acceleration to achieve real-time processing at 30+ FPS.

Low-Latency Communication

Challenge: Sending detection events to the server instantly without disrupting the interview flow or causing delays.

Solution: WebSocket connections for real-time communication, event queuing, and optimized data structures to minimize payload size.

Cross-Device Compatibility

Challenge: Ensuring consistent AI performance across different devices, browsers, and hardware configurations.

Solution: Adaptive model loading, device capability detection, and graceful degradation for lower-end hardware while maintaining core functionality.

Solution: Multi-model validation, confidence thresholds, temporal analysis, and behavior pattern recognition to improve accuracy.
